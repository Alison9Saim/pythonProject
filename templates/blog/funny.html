Funny "Would You Rather" Questions

Lighthearted and hilarious scenarios to make you laugh.

  • Would you rather always talk in rhymes or sing everything you say?
  • Would you rather have spaghetti for hair or meatballs for eyes?
  • Would you rather fight one horse-sized duck or 100 duck-sized horses?
  • Would you rather sneeze glitter or burp confetti?
  • Would you rather wear clown shoes every day or a clown nose forever?
  • Would you rather only be able to whisper or only be able to shout?
  • Would you rather have a pet dinosaur or a pet dragon?
  • Would you rather always forget people’s names or always forget where you parked?
  • Would you rather eat ice cream with ketchup or pizza with chocolate sauce?
  • Would you rather be stuck in a room full of mosquitos or a room full of pigeons?
  • Would you rather have to hop everywhere or crawl everywhere?
  • Would you rather laugh uncontrollably at serious moments or cry at funny ones?
  • Would you rather be able to teleport but only to bathrooms, or fly but only 2 feet off the ground?
  • Would you rather have your life narrated by Morgan Freeman or by a sports commentator?
  • Would you rather wear a tutu to work every day or a superhero cape everywhere you go?
