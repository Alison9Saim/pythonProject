How to Submit Your Own Questions

Published on Nov 17, 2025

Would You Rather taps into curiosity and social comparison. People love seeing how their choices stack up against others. The game creates instant conversation starters, sparks debates, and reveals hidden preferences. Psychologists call this “decision play” — a safe way to explore values without real‑world consequences. That’s why it’s so engaging: every choice feels meaningful, even when it’s silly.

Coming Soon: Submit Your Own Questions

We’re excited to announce that soon, you will be able to submit your own hilarious, thought‑provoking, or downright tricky “Would You Rather” questions directly into the game.

Once submitted, your questions will be added to the pool for other players to enjoy and respond to. This means the game will grow with community creativity, making every round more surprising and fun.

The submission form and functionality aren’t live yet — but we’re actively working on it next. Stay tuned for updates!

Community Guidelines

To keep the game fun and safe for everyone, all submitted questions will be reviewed. If a question is found to be inappropriate, offensive, or breaks our rules, it will be removed from the game and the website.

We encourage creativity and humor, but please keep submissions respectful and suitable for all players.
