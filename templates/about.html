About This Game

Welcome to Would You Rather — a simple but addictive game where you choose between two options and see how your choice compares with everyone else’s. It’s a fun way to spark conversations, challenge your instincts, and laugh at the surprising results.

How It Works

  • You’ll be presented with a question and two possible answers.
  • Pick the option you prefer — Option A or Option B.
  • See how many other players agreed with you.
  • Earn points for picking the more popular choice!

Why We Built It

We wanted to create a lighthearted game that anyone can play in seconds. Whether you’re killing time, debating with friends, or just curious about what people think, this game is designed to entertain and connect.

Features

  • Randomized questions pulled from our game database.
  • Instant feedback on how your choice compares.
  • Funny “loss messages” when you pick the less popular option.
  • Simple scoring system to keep track of your streaks.

Future Plans

We’re working on adding more questions, themed categories, and multiplayer modes so you can challenge your friends directly. Stay tuned!
